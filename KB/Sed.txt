====== Sed ======
Created Thursday 21 November 2013

**replaces any number of white spaces with just one**
sed "s/^ *//;s/ *$//;s/ \{1,\}/ /g"

s/^ *// deletes spaces at the start of the line
s/ *$// deletes spaces at the end of the line
