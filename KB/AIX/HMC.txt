====== HMC ======
Created Wednesday 15 January 2014

**Get access to ASMI**

	**List all managed processor by HMC:**
	lssysconn -r all -F type_model_serial_num:ipaddr:state

	**redirect 443 from ASMI through HMC(on localhost)**
	ssh -N -L 8080:MANAGED_PROC_IP:443 plp70065@HMC_IP

	**ASMI is now on https://localhost:8080**
